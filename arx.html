Light from non objects

ARX #731

Appling material #2637u5 to Supershape [5e] multiple experiments have confirmed that upon drastic change in velocity, we see an equally drastic change in the object’s luminous components. Unexpectedly, studies also confirm that upon collision of physical surfaces, visual and audial feedback occurs from unknown environmental sources at a measurement of 0.00056m/s.
